Who We Are

A Farm Brand
Built on Integrity

SasyaEarth is a vertically integrated agri-food enterprise — we own the farmland, grow the crop, process the product, and build the brand. From seed to shelf, every step is ours.

Our Technology

Farming Powered by Intelligence

We bring global agri-tech to every acre — ensuring consistency, quality and efficiency that traditional farming simply cannot match.

📡
IoT Soil Sensor Network
Real-time soil moisture, pH, temperature and nitrogen per acre. Automated drip irrigation triggered only when and where needed — saving water significantly vs flood irrigation.
🚁
Drone Crop Surveillance
Weekly UAV surveys detect crop stress, pest infestation and growth anomalies early. Early detection dramatically reduces crop loss versus traditional manual scouting.
🤖
AI Disease Detection
Computer vision models analyse drone imagery to identify over 40 crop diseases with high accuracy. Farm managers are alerted within 2 hours of detection.
🔍
Farm-to-Fork Digital Traceability
QR-code traceability per batch — scanned from our farm right through to retail or customs. Full transparency for every buyer and consumer in our chain.
❄️
Smart Cold Chain Logistics
IoT-monitored refrigerated transport extends shelf life significantly vs traditional methods. Critical for maintaining quality on the journey to international markets.

Our Commitment

Farming for the Future

Every decision we make is guided by our responsibility to the soil, the farmer, and the consumer — today and for generations to come.

💧
Water Conservation
Drip irrigation delivers water precisely to each plant's root zone, dramatically reducing water consumption compared to traditional flood irrigation. We are committed to groundwater conservation in our farming region.
🌱
Zero Chemical Policy
No synthetic fertilizers. No pesticides. No herbicides. Ever. We use organic composts, crop rotation, and biological pest management to produce food that is pure, safe and nutritious.
🌾
Soil Restoration
Organic inputs and crop rotation actively restore soil health over time — reversing the degradation caused by decades of chemical-intensive farming in the region.
👨‍🌾
Farmer Empowerment
Our contract farming model guarantees prices above the government's Minimum Support Price — ensuring income dignity for every farmer in our network. We create direct and indirect employment across the supply chain.
📉
Carbon Light Farming
By eliminating chemical fertilizers (a major source of agricultural greenhouse gas emissions), using drip irrigation to reduce pump energy, and growing no paddy or sugarcane, our farm has a significantly lighter carbon footprint.
🔍
Full Traceability
Every batch of our produce carries a QR code that traces it back to the exact farm, harvest date, soil test and crop cycle. Transparency is not a feature — it is our promise.
